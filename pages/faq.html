🎴 About Oripalot 3 questions
What is Oripalot?
Oripalot is a Japan-based online oripa (digital trading card lottery) platform. Players purchase coin packs and use those coins to enter draws for sealed trading card products — such as Pokemon, Yu-Gi-Oh!, and more. Unlike physical card packs, oripa draws determine your prize digitally, with the physical card shipped to your door.
Is Oripalot free to play?
You can join Oripalot for free and receive a welcome bonus of Free Coins to try your first draws at no cost. However, most premium oripa draws require purchasing Oripa Coins. Free Coins are also awarded through daily quests, campaigns, and level-up bonuses.
What countries can I play from?
Oripalot is currently available to residents of Japan only. Physical prize shipping is limited to Japanese domestic addresses. We plan to expand to additional regions in the future — follow our official LINE and X accounts for updates.
🪙 Coins & Currency 4 questions
What are Oripa Coins?
Oripa Coins (🪙) are the primary paid currency on Oripalot. They are purchased with Japanese Yen and can be used for any oripa draw. 1 Oripa Coin = ¥1 in draw value. Purchased coins do not expire as long as your account remains active.
What are Free Coins?
Free Coins (💚) are bonus coins earned through:
  • Registration bonus
  • Daily quest completions
  • Level-up rewards
  • Campaign and event bonuses
Free Coins can be used on designated free-coin-eligible draws only. They cannot be withdrawn or exchanged for cash.
Do coins expire?
Oripa Coins purchased with real money do not expire while your account is in good standing. Free Coins expire 90 days after they are issued. You can always check your coin expiry dates in your Wallet page.
How do I check my coin balance?
Your coin balances are always displayed in the top navigation bar on every page. For a full breakdown including transaction history and expiry dates, visit your Wallet page.
🎲 Playing Oripa Draws 4 questions
How do Oripa draws work?
Each oripa draw is a sealed digital lottery. A pool of prizes is set before the draw opens. When you enter a draw, a random prize from that pool is assigned to you. The result is determined the moment you draw — there is no manipulation after purchase. Your prize is then held in your Prize Box until you request shipping.
What is the payout ratio (RTP)?
Oripalot targets a payout ratio of 75–90% across all draws, meaning the total market value of prizes in a draw pool represents at least 75% of the total entry cost for that pool. Individual RTP varies by draw type and is displayed on each draw's detail page.
Can I draw multiple times?
Yes! Most draws allow single draws and multi-draws (e.g. 5x, 10x at a time). Multi-draws are processed simultaneously and may offer a small discount. Each individual entry is still a separate random outcome from the prize pool.
What happens when all entries are sold?
When the last entry in a draw pool is sold, the draw closes automatically. Any unsold entries that expire (for time-limited draws) are removed from the pool and the draw closes early. The final remaining prize is awarded to the final draw entrant.
📦 Prizes & Shipping 5 questions
How do I receive my prize?
Won prizes are stored in your Prize Box. To receive them physically, go to Prize Box → select the items → tap "Request Shipping." You can consolidate multiple prizes into one shipment. Once dispatched, you'll receive a tracking number by email.
How long does shipping take?
Standard domestic shipping within Japan typically takes 3–7 business days after your shipping request is processed. Processing takes 1–3 business days. Express options may be available for an additional fee.
What if my prize expires before I request shipping?
Prizes in your Prize Box are held for 90 days from the date won. You will receive reminder notifications at 30 days and 7 days before expiry. If a prize expires unclaimed, it cannot be recovered. We strongly recommend requesting shipping promptly.
Can I convert prizes to coins?
Yes! Most prizes can be converted to Oripa Coins instead of shipped. The coin conversion rate is shown on each prize in your Prize Box (typically 30–60% of the prize's market value). Converted coins are added to your balance immediately.
What is the minimum for shipping requests?
There is no minimum number of prizes required to request shipping. However, a flat shipping fee of ¥550 (incl. tax) applies per shipment regardless of how many items are included. Bundling multiple prizes saves on shipping costs.
💳 Payments & Purchases 3 questions
What payment methods are accepted?
Oripalot accepts:
  • Credit/Debit Cards — Visa, Mastercard, JCB, AMEX
  • Google Pay
  • Apple Pay
  • Convenience Store payment — Lawson, FamilyMart, 7-Eleven, Ministop
All transactions are processed securely in Japanese Yen (JPY).
Are there purchase limits?
Yes. To promote responsible play, Oripalot applies the following monthly limits:
  • Standard accounts: ¥100,000 / month
  • KYC-verified accounts: ¥300,000 / month
  • Users under 18: ¥10,000 / month
Limits reset on the 1st of each month (JST).
Can I get a refund?
Coin purchases are generally non-refundable once the coins have been used in a draw. Unused coins from a purchase may be refunded within 8 days of purchase if you have not used them, subject to verification. Please contact support for refund requests.
🪪 Account & KYC 3 questions
When is KYC required?
KYC (Know Your Customer) verification is required when:
  • Your cumulative purchases exceed ¥50,000
  • You request a shipping address for prizes
  • You wish to increase your monthly purchase limit above ¥100,000
KYC is processed within 1–2 business days.
What ID documents are accepted for KYC?
We accept any of the following Japanese government-issued documents:
  • 運転免許証 (Driver's License)
  • マイナンバーカード (My Number Card)
  • パスポート (Passport)
  • 健康保険証 (Health Insurance Card) + supplemental address document
Documents must be current and clearly photographed.
What are the rules for users under 18?
Users under 18 years of age are subject to stricter controls:
  • Monthly purchase limit: ¥10,000
  • Parental consent must be confirmed at registration
  • Access to certain premium draws may be restricted
  • KYC with parental guardian information is required before first purchase
These rules comply with Japanese consumer protection law.
